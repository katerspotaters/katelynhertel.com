Willow's Treasures

This was my second commissioned site made for my sister's jewelry making business. Willow's Treasures is a Mother and Daughter company that features one of a kind wire wrap beaded bracelets and earrings. Each piece of jewelry is unique with eye catching glass and crystal beads from around the world.

Using HTML and CSS I included a photo page and links to all her upcoming craft shows. There is also a small blurb about the business as well as an award they've won.
